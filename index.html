About

01 / Profile

I design the outside plant networks that carry broadband into homes and businesses across the United States and beyond — the splice diagrams, optical budgets, routing plans, and construction-ready engineering packages that determine whether a fiber rollout actually works in the field, not just on paper.

Over 9 years across CCI Systems and Cyient Limited, I've delivered FTTH/FTTx, GPON, and HFC network design for major US carrier programs — including 404+ miles of FTTH designs at 98%+ accuracy for Mediacom's BEAD rural broadband expansion, large-scale HFC migration for Astound Wave, RDOF-funded fiber builds for Altice, and BAU design operations for CenturyLink/Lumen. My work has also extended internationally, supporting GPON/UFB rollouts for Ventia in New Zealand and FTTP greenfield design for Broad Spectrum in Australia.

My focus is the layer most designers skip past: QA/QC rigor and geo-accuracy validation. I build process and mentor teams specifically to catch errors before they cost a client time and money in the field — a discipline that improved design accuracy by 25% for teams I've trained.
